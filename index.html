About

Dr. Norman W Allen is a Cardiovascular Disease (cardiology) Specialist in Washington, District of Columbia. He graduated with honors in 1984. Having more than 30 years of diverse experiences, especially in Cardiovascular Disease (Cardiology), Dr. Norman W Allen affiliates with many hospitals including Providence Hospital Center, Prince George's Hospital Center Medstar Hospital Center, and cooperates with other doctors in many medical groups including Dimensions Healthcare Associates Inc., All Heart Medical Center.
